Walkie Talkie

by managore - Compo Entry

Send messages

Receive messages

Play messages



/howtoplay

Use the <arrow keys>, <page up> and <page down> to navigate to a message, then press <enter> to play the message. Use the <arrow keys> and <space> (or a gamepad) to run and jump your way to the green circle to beat the message, or press <esc> to go back to navigating. You can hold down <page up> or <page down> to quickly navigate through the messages.

Every two minutes you can press <tab> to switch between navigating messages and typing a message. Use any and all of the keyboard keys to write your message, then press <enter> to play your message. As soon as you beat your message, it gets sent and other people will see it. If you want to change your message, press <esc> to go back to typing. Make sure not to beat your message if you don't want to send it yet!

You can press <alt> + <enter> to toggle fullscreen, and you can press <esc> once to change your username and a second time to exit the game.

/help

- You need an internet connection to get new messages
- If your messages aren't showing up after a few seconds (and you've scrolled down all the way), please let me know
- A green circle after a level means you've beaten it, a red heart after a level means I think it's particularly fun or unique

/about

Made with GameMaker:Studio, Aseprite and Sublime Text 2. The font used is m5x7 (you can download it for free at https://managore.itch.io/m5x7). The user interface style is inspired by Aseprite's beautiful UI. Many thanks to @takorii for letting me use their server, without it the game couldn't exist.

The post compo build is functionally the same, but lets you search for levels (<f3> or <ctrl> + <f>), mark a level as a favourite of yours (<f2> or <ctrl> + <s>) and quickly skip to the oldest or newest level (<home> and <end> respectively).
